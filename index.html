THE UNBALANCED GALAXY DISCORD SERVER RULES

Last Updated 12/31/2025, 8:42PM CST – 380 Members at the time
⚖️ Server Rules
Section A. General Conduct +

Part A. Respect Everyone

All members must treat others with respect. Harassment, insults, threats, or intentionally causing drama are not tolerated.

Part B. No Hate or Discrimination

Racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, or any form of hateful behavior is strictly prohibited regardless of intent.

Part C. Common Sense Rule

If an action feels harmful, disruptive, or unsafe, assume it is not allowed even if not explicitly listed.

Section B. Discord Platform Rules +

Part A. Discord Terms of Service

All Discord Terms of Service and Community Guidelines apply and are enforced within this server.

Part B. Illegal Content

Any illegal activity, including piracy, exploits, or distribution of illegal material, is forbidden.

Part C. NSFW Restrictions

NSFW content, sexual material, extreme violence, or gore is not allowed in any channel.

Section C. Channel Usage +

Part A. Usage of Channels

Use channels only for their intended purpose. Off-topic discussion should be redirected appropriately.

Part B. Spam and Flooding

Spam is only permitted in designated channels. Message flooding, copy-pasting, or mic spam is prohibited.

Part C. Bot and Game Channels

Do not abuse bots, break counting games, or intentionally disrupt automated systems.

Section D. Promotion & Advertising +

Part A. Self-Promotion

Advertising and self-promotion are not allowed unless explicitly approved or posted in the designated channel.

Part B. Server Invites

Posting server invites without permission is prohibited, including indirect or disguised promotions.

Part C. External Links

Links that are malicious, misleading, or unsafe will result in moderation action.

Section E. Profiles & Identity +

Part A. Usernames and Avatars

Usernames, avatars, and bios must be appropriate and not impersonate other members or staff.

Part B. Impersonation

Impersonating staff, bots, or other users is strictly forbidden.

Part C. Personal Information

Do not share private or sensitive information about yourself or others.

Section F. Staff & Moderation +

Part A. Respect Staff

Staff decisions must be respected. If you disagree, use tickets or appropriate support channels.

Part B. No Mini-Modding

Do not act as staff or enforce rules yourself. Report issues instead.

Part C. Do Not Ask for Mod

Staff roles are earned through trust and contribution, not requested.

Section G. Tickets, Events, and Voice +

Part A. Ticket Usage

Tickets must be used for legitimate support, reports, or questions. Troll or ghost tickets are not allowed.

Part B. Voice Chat Conduct

Voice chats follow the same rules as text channels. Be respectful and avoid disruptive behavior.

Part C. Events

Follow all event instructions. Cheating or exploiting will result in removal or disqualification.

Section H. Enforcement & Alts +

Part A. No Ban Evasion

Using alternate accounts to bypass punishments is strictly prohibited.

Part B. Punishments

Rule violations may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, or bans depending on severity.

Part C. Final Authority

Staff reserve the right to take action in situations not explicitly covered by these rules.

These rules are a starter framework and will evolve as the server grows.
